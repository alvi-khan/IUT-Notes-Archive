Task

Discuss the extent to which you agree or disagree with the statement and explain your reasoning for the position you take. In developing and supporting your position, you should consider ways in which the statement might or might not hold true and explain how those considerations shape your position. 

A simple guideline for structuring your essay can be found here: https://www.kaptest.com/study/gre/how-to-structure-the-gre-issue-essay/
